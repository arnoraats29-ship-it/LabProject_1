FAQ

Your questions answered

Have other questions that you don’t see in our FAQ? You can find all the details
on our contact page, where you can reach out to us directly.

What is Fumble and how does it work? +

Fumble is a student platform where members exchange study help, notes and tutoring in a simple and fair system.

What types of tasks or help can be exchanged on Fumble? +

Students can exchange notes, summaries, tutoring sessions and help with specific study tasks.

How does Fumble ensure academic integrity? +

The platform encourages learning support only. Cheating is not allowed and can be reported.

Do I need to pay to use the platform? +

Basic use of Fumble is free for students. Optional extras may cost money.

Can I participate if I’m not a member of the school? +

Some communities are school-only, others are open. This depends on the group rules.
